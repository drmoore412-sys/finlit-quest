## Preserved Approved Reserve Definitions

### SAVINGS

An account designed to hold money for future needs and potentially earn interest.

### RECURRING

Describes a payment or transfer scheduled to happen repeatedly.

These approved definitions are preserved without alteration. They do not convert either reserve term into an active v1.1 playable answer.

## CLEAR and CLEARING Distinction

**CLEAR** remains active and means:

> To complete the information-processing stage required before a payment can be settled.

**CLEARING** remains reserved and represents the broader operational process.
